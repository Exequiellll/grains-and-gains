Our story

Cooked for the long day you just had.

Grains & Gains started as a small kitchen off the New Bypass — a place for jeepney drivers, nurses on break, students between exams, and tired parents picking up dinner on the way home. Our rule from day one was simple: nobody leaves hungry, and nobody leaves broke.

Everything is cooked in small batches throughout the day. The sinigang broth is built from scratch. The tapa is marinated overnight. The rice is fluffed the second it finishes steaming. If we run out of something, we close the pot — we don't reheat yesterday onto today's plate.
